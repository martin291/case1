About Me

Hello and welcome to my page. I'm Martin!

I'm from Dublin, Ireland. I'm in my first year at DCU, studying Computer Applications. I'm enjoying the course and have made quite a few new friends. I don't have that much prior experience with HTML and CSS however, I feel I am getting the hang of it very quickly.

When I'm not doing college work I like to spend my time listening to music, watching movies, playing video games and chatting to friends over Discord. I don't play sports but I try to stay fit and healthy. I go on walks often and I like learning new recipes to cook or bake. I also enjoy art but I wouldn't say I'm very good at it. I sometimes follow Bob Ross tutorials or draw portraits of people.

I am enjoying college but I miss my secondary school. TY was a very fun year. I went on school trips to Germany and the West of Ireland. I enjoyed the weeks of work experience I got in TY.


Work Experience

During TY I spent weeks working in a few different places:

  • I worked for a week at NearFM Radio Station. I sat in with the radio presenters and observed them work. Each day a different volunteer would have a show prepared. I learnt what each button and switch on the control panel did. For example:

    1. How to fade in out of a song
    2. How to play a song from a CD or through Spotify
    3. How to take live calls

  • I worked for two weeks at the Office of Public Works State Art Collection . I spent that time going to several Art exhibitions, organising folders and unboxing paintings and transporting art pieces.

  • I spent half a week at Ericcson learning Scratch and some basic Python with my peers. We worked in groups of four, designing a game in scratch.

  • I worked at PC Moore & Company Solicitors for about a week. I spent most of the time reading books on law or answering phone calls. However, on the second last day I was brought to the Four Courts to be shown around. We spent the entire day taking the Luas around town.
