Product Information Section By Shane Dixon

About Our Product

Crafted with the environment and people in mind, Feezer is a delicious eco-friendly beverage which is natural to both the environment and our bodies.

With use of natural recipes we offer flavours varying from strawberry, raspberry and even watermelon! Our all organic recipes would mean that there wouldn't be a lot of sugar and that it is also vegan friendly.

We use a biopolymer plastic for our bottles in order to counteract the pollution left by regular polymer plastic bottles. A biopolymer plastic bottle uses nature to its own advantage in production hence the environment doesn’t suffer!

We have three main focuses:

  1. Customer Satisfaction
  2. Nutrition
  3. Saving the Environment

We at Feezer believe that businesses can run smoothly without hurting the environment which is why we set forward in innovating newer and more modern plastics.
